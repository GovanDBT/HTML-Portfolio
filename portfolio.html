App that measures Carbon Emission (2023)

In 2023, i took part in IBM's global challenge who's theme was sustainable tech where we were tasked with coming up with tech solutions while applying IBM AI tools that could fight climate change, reduce food crisis, improve supply of clean water to communities, etc. For my project i decided to come up with an app that could give users information about the amount of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, measure their carbon emission and get assistant from an AI tool called Watson Assistant. The project was built using JavaScript, React-Native and PHP.

Gaborone City Crime locating website (2021)

For my System Programming class during my second year we were tasked with building a website that could help keep track of crime and identify crime hot-spots in the city of Gaborone. In our website we implemented a Map from Goggle Maps that users can use to see their locations, see places were a crime like theft, burglary, robbery, trafficking, arson, grand-theft or homicide took place, see when the crime was committed, inform users that a crime took place near them, and could even identify to users road accidents that occurred near them. The website was meant to help users avoid places that were potential dangerous and see the growth of crime in their region. We used HTML, CSS and PHP for the project.
